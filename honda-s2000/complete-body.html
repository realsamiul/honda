  Unveiled in 1999 to mark Hondas 50th anniversary, the S2000 was built to embody racing technology in a street-legal package. Its high-revving engine and lightweight chassis made it a standout in the sports car world.

Engineering Meets Artistry

Every line and curve of the S2000 was sculpted for both performance and beauty. The long hood, short deck, and cockpit-forward stance create a silhouette thats as functional as it is elegant.

Inside, the minimalist interior puts the driver front and center, with controls designed for intuitive use. The S2000s design is a perfect balance of form and function, built to enhance the connection between car and driver.

The original S2000 arrived with a 2.0L F20C engine producing 240 hp and a screaming 9,000 rpm redline, one of the highest for any production car. Paired with a lightweight chassis, and a near-perfect 50:50 weight distribution, the AP1 delivered a raw, track-ready experience straight from the showroom.

ap1 - modified

The AP2 refined the formula. The engine was updated to a 2.2L F22C1 in North America, offering more torque and a lower 8,200 rpm redline for improved drivability. Suspension geometry was revised for better stability, and subtle design tweaks gave the car a more mature, planted feel without losing its edge.

pure analog thrill

pure analog thrill

The S2000s 6-speed manual is legendary for its short-throw, precise, and beautifully weighted.

Paired with a high-revving engine and near-perfect balance, it delivers an engaging, connected drive.

No electronic aids, just raw feedback and pure joy behind the wheel. Its a car that rewards skill and passion, making every drive memorable.

under the surface

under the surface

under the surface

under the surface

The S2000s chassis features a rigid X-bone structure, designed for strength and near-perfect balance.

Its double wishbone suspension at all four corners delivers sharp handling and precise road feedback, tuned for both street and track.

Under the hood, the F20C and F22C engines are hand-assembled by Hondas master technicians.

Built with racing principles in mind, they offer high-revving power, reliability, and one of the most engaging naturally aspirated driving experiences ever made.

The S2000 is more than a car, its a symbol of purity in design and driving. In today's digital world, it remains defiantly analog, connecting driver and machine in every mile.

Its influence continues to inspire new generations of drivers and designers who value authenticity and engagement over convenience and automation.
